About Us

ABOUT CEED INTERNATIONAL Read about us and be familiar with us...!

CEED INTERNATIONAL stands out as an outstanding online and offline platform offering a comprehensive array of services, including counseling, training, undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), certificate, and diploma courses. Recognized as the premier institute, it provides Montessori teacher training courses for over 3000 students. The institute accomplishes this through collaborations with various academic associates and boasts a multitude of programs and innovative ventures in psychological research. Situated in Kasaragod, Kerala, India, CEED INTERNATIONAL exemplifies excellence in education and professional development

What We Do?

Collaboration via Memorandum of Understanding

We have a host of institutions which have entrusted our service and come to a memorandum of understanding with us after proper formal consultations. It is our policy to share our expertise to other institutions that possess similar interests and manpower.

Placement Assistance

It is a service to get job aspirants in touch with recent job opportunities; to host workshops to face job interviews and aptitude tests. Candidates are taught resume writing, skill development and time management. We also recruit candidates with outstanding profile at different positions in CEED International and others reputed firms and enterprises.

Preschool Curriculum in Your Brand

This is a service provided by us to help schools develop a curriculum of their own, meeting some of the stringent international standards. The panorama of experience we have gained in preparing syllabi for various pre-schools in India is a strength that cements our confidence to go ahead steadily.
